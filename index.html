How RoleLang Works

Why I Made This

Language learning isn't just about memorizing vocabulary or grammar rules. Real fluency comes from practicing actual conversations in realistic situations, over and over again. That's why I created RoleLang - to give you unlimited opportunities to practice real-world scenarios with an AI partner who never gets tired of helping you improve.

How to Use

1

Choose your target language and enter a roleplay topic (or pick from our suggestions)

2

AI generates a realistic conversation scenario with an illustration

3

Listen to your lines first, then use the microphone to practice speaking

4

Get instant feedback on your pronunciation and continue the conversation

Key Features

Voice recognition practice
AI-generated illustrations
8+ languages supported
Lesson history & review
Vocabulary quizzes
Adjustable audio speed

Pro Tips

  • Practice the same scenario multiple times to build muscle memory
  • Use slower audio speeds when starting, then gradually increase
  • Review previous lessons and take vocabulary quizzes to reinforce learning
  • Try different scenarios in the same language to expand your vocabulary
